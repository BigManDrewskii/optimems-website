## 9. FAQ Section

### Headline
**Partnership Program FAQs**

### Common Questions

#### Q: What types of businesses do you partner with?
**A:** We partner with System Integrators, Consultants, Building Developers, Software Resellers, Equipment Distributors, and Equipment Manufacturers who serve the energy management sector.

#### Q: What are the financial requirements to become a partner?
**A:** Financial requirements vary by partnership type. We'll discuss specific investment requirements during the application process. Generally, partners should be financially stable and able to invest in training and initial marketing.

#### Q: How long does the application process take?
**A:** The application review typically takes 5 business days. The complete onboarding process, from application to full partner activation, usually takes 3-4 weeks.

#### Q: Do you offer exclusive territory rights?
**A:** Territorial exclusivity is available in select markets for qualified partners who meet specific performance criteria. We'll discuss territory options during the discovery call.

#### Q: What kind of training and support do you provide?
**A:** We provide comprehensive technical training, sales enablement, marketing support, and ongoing technical assistance. Partners have access to our partner portal with 24/7 resources.

#### Q: What are the revenue opportunities?
**A:** Partners can generate revenue through hardware sales, software subscriptions, professional services, and ongoing maintenance contracts. Specific margin structures are discussed during the onboarding process.

#### Q: Can I partner with Optimems if I'm already working with competitors?
**A:** We evaluate each partnership opportunity individually. We're looking for partners committed to mutual success, which may include specific performance commitments.

#### Q: How is marketing support provided?
**A:** We provide co-branded marketing materials, lead generation support, joint press releases, case study development, and event co-hosting opportunities.
